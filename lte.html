5G LTE vs Fibre

Is 5G LTE Right for You?

5G LTE is the perfect choice where fibre isn't available, or if you need internet quickly without waiting for an installation.

✅ Choose 5G LTE if…

  • 📍Fibre isn't available at your address
  • ⚡You need internet today — no installation wait
  • 🚗You move around or rent (no infrastructure commitment)
  • 💼You need a backup line for when fibre goes down
  • 🏕️You're in a semi-rural or peri-urban area
  • 💰You want lower upfront costs (no installation fee)

🚀 Consider Fibre if…

  • 🏠Fibre is available at your home or business
  • 📶You need symmetrical upload and download speeds
  • 👨‍💻You work from home with heavy cloud usage
  • 🎮You game competitively or stream 4K on multiple screens
  • 👨‍👩‍👧‍👦You have 4+ people using internet simultaneously

The stuff you need to know about 5G LTE:
  • Month-To-Month — No lock-in, cancel anytime with 1 calendar month's notice.
  • 5G/LTE Router Required — Data SIMs require specific routers per network and do not work with older routers. Once-off purchase: blazing-fast 5G router R2,999 or plug & play LTE router R2,000. WhatsApp Brits to find out which router you need before ordering.
  • Signal matters — Put your router near a window for best results. Don't stuff it in a cupboard.
  • Weak signal? No problem — Boost it with an external 5G LTE antenna. 2-cable: R2,500 · 4-cable: R3,500. Game changer for fringe areas!
  • 5G/LTE Speeds are "up to" and depend on signal strength, tower distance and network load. All 5G/LTE is a best-effort service.
  • No roaming — These SIMs are for use in South Africa only. Need international data? Ask about our Airalo & Holafy eSIMs.
