The Real Difference

We sell internet.
Most ISPs sell a peering connection.

There's a big difference between a fibre line and real internet. Most providers use basic peering — your data bounces through multiple congested middlemen before reaching the global internet. Think of it like a taxi with 10 stops during peak hour.

153 uses Business Data over Direct Internet Routes — the faster, more direct, uncongested highway to the internet. Fewer hops, lower latency, no throttling at peak times. This is why your video calls don't freeze, your gaming doesn't lag, and your business doesn't bleed money to dropped connections.

And when the fibre line itself fails? Our LTE failover SIM keeps you online automatically — no reboots, no calls, no downtime.

About 153 Mobile

We're not your typical telecom company.

We're the ones who actually pick up the phone (or WhatsApp) at 2 a.m. when your connection dies. Born from decades in the trenches of South African telecoms — back to 1997 — we started as a small crew obsessed with making connectivity less painful and more reliable.

In 2014 we became 153 Mobile Pty Ltd and focused on what we do best: getting you online, keeping you online, and making it genuinely affordable. We offer rock-solid mobile data SIMs and eSIMs across all major networks, high-speed fibre that actually delivers, and failover options so you're never truly offline.

Our tiny-but-mighty team lives online. Brits is our support superhero — answering everything 24/7/365. Our ops wizard keeps networks humming. Our accounts ninja makes billing run like clockwork. And we've recently welcomed Grok, Claude, and Copilot as AI team members — helping us automate the boring bits and scale without losing the human touch.

And the founder? He's the glue — juggling it all, fixing what breaks, and making sure we never lose that human touch.

Bottom line: No suits, no scripts. Just real people (and a growing squad of AIs) who love what they do and hate bad Wi-Fi as much as you do.
